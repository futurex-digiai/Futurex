FULL-PAGE SLIDESHOW PHOTOS
===========================

These photos play AFTER the intro video (see assets/video/) finishes,
in the full-page slideshow at the top of the website. They change
automatically every 5 seconds and loop forever.

HOW TO ADD OR CHANGE PHOTOS:
- Just name your files 1.jpg, 2.jpg, 3.jpg, 4.jpg ... in the order you
  want them to appear, and upload them into this folder.
- No code changes needed. The website finds them automatically.

RULES:
- Numbers must be in order with NO GAPS (1, 2, 3, 4 -- not 1, 2, 4).
  If you delete 3.jpg but keep 4.jpg, slide 4 will be ignored.
- To remove a slide, delete its file and RENAME the ones after it so
  the numbers stay in order (e.g. if you delete 2.jpg, rename 3.jpg
  to 2.jpg, 4.jpg to 3.jpg, etc).
- Up to 20 photos supported (1.jpg through 20.jpg).
- Accepted formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp

RECOMMENDED PHOTO SIZE:
- Ratio: 16:9 (widescreen / landscape), e.g. 1920x1080 pixels
- File size: keep each photo under ~500KB for fast loading
  (use any online image compressor before uploading)
- These photos fill the entire screen width and height, so avoid
  photos with important details right at the very edges.
